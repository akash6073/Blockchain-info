Difference between Blockchain & Crypto

Inherent Nature :-

Blockchain is a storage technology of data on decentralized networks. Cryptocurrency is a medium of exchange like US Dollars.
Blockchain can be used for storing other types of data as well.

Monetary Value :-

All Cryptocurrenies have monetary value, whereas Blockchain does not have any monetary value.

Mobility :-

Blockchain technology is decentralized and distributed all over the world. there is no single location where all records are stored.
Cryptocurrencies, although held in blockchain can be accessed via mobile wallets.
If you have a Bitcoin wallet, you can use it anywhere for transacting with parties accepting Bitcoin.

Usage :-

Blockchain technology has usage beyond Cryptocurrencies.
It can be used for recording transactions in banking, healthcare, supply chain & retail.
Cryptocurrency is digital money used for buying goods & for investment.

Transparency :-

Blockchain being a public ledger, is highly transparent, Anyone can join a blockchain network and view the information avilable.
On other hand, cryptocurrencies offer anonymity.
So, anyone can see source/destination of Bitcoin transaction, yet no one can know who is behaind the transaction.
