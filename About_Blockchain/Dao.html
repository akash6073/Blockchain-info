These groups of like-minded people come together from all world, to build smart contracts that they lay out the foundational rules and execute agreed-upon decisions.

More About DAOs

How to join DAO

Those who wish to become members often buy their way into the DAO by purchasing a governance token for the desired DAO.

Holding a governance token gives community members the ablity to create proposals for the DAO and to vote on those proposals.

Advantage of a DAOs

An advantage of a DAOs framework is the alignment of incentives. Meaning, it's in the individual's best intrest to honestly vote for what the believe will serve the protocol best.

In a DAO, there is no one person who can authorize spending based on their own desires, and no way to secretly manipulae the books. Everything is out in the open, and the DAO's rules are written in the code for everyone to see.

A successful DAO

A successful DAO will see more usage, and eventually, see an increase in the value of its token. So when the protocol succeeds so will the token holders.

Types of DAOs

GRANT DAOs :-

A Grant DAO is where members of their community donate funds into a grant pool and vote on how to spend those funds.

these DAOs are often used to fund new innovative Defi Project.

SOCIAL DAOs :-

A Social DAO is often built to focus on a particular object, like preserving arts and culture or pursuing projects that resonate with their community values.

DAOs is a super powerful application which would provide security for your crypto or web3 assets.
