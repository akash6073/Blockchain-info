Why DCA

DCA is designed to help offset any negative effect on an investment caused by short-term market volatility.

if the price of an asset drops during the time you are dollar-cost averaging, then you stand to make a profit if the price moves back up.

Dollar-cost averaging refers to the practice of systematically investing equal amounts, spaced out over regular intervals, regardless of price.

More on investopedia

How Does DCA Work

Let's take a simple example to understand how DCA works.

Assume you have decide to invest $15,000 in a token priced at $100. At this price, you will end up with 150 tokens.

Now, let say you decide to spread $15,000 over five months. you invest $3,000 every month, and to your surprise, the price starts falling in the following months. it goes from $100 to $85, $70, $80, $90, and $100. Now you will end up with 168.74 tokens.

With DCA, you end up getting more value for your money.

What Are Some Drawbacks of DCA ?

A disadvantage of dollar-cost averaging is that the market tends to go up over time.

This means that if you invest a lump sum earlier, it is likely to do better than smaller amounts invested over a period of time.

The lump sum will provide a better return over the long run as a result of the market’s rising tendency.

Dollar-cost averaging is not a solution for all investment risks, however. You will have to take on the task of identifying good investments and do your research even if you opt for a passive dollar-cost averaging approach.

If the investment you identify turns out to be a bad pick, you will only be investing steadily into a losing investment.

Pro & Cons of DCA

Is a DCA strategy viable for Crypto ?

Cryptocurrencies can be quite volatile, often-times even more so than stocks.

You can generate a potentially greater profit from buying during dips and selling at the top.

However, DCA is a safer method of investing than lump sum buying and selling. it's lower risk and lower reward, but still offers the chance of benefiting from market swings.

DOLLAR-COST AVERAGING is a tried and tested investment strategy that works extremely well in the case of crypto currencies, especially during a bear market.
