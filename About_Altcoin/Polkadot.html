About Polkadot Network

Polkadot is an open source blockchain plateform and cryptocurrency.

it provides interconnectivity and interoperability between blockchains.

Polkadot network has a primary blockchain named "relay chain" and many user-created parallel chains called "parachains"

it's designed to be speedy and scalable, via the use of many parachains.

More About Polkadot

The Polkadot mission

Polkadot envision a Web where our identity and our data is our own - safely secured from any central authority.

Polkadot will enable a completely decentralized web where users are in control.

Polkadot facilitates an internet where independent blockchains can exchange information and transactions in a trustless way via the Polkadot relay chain.

About (DOT) Coin

DOT is native token of POLKADOT network.

Polkadot token (DOT) serves 3 main functions within the network:

  • it's a governance token
  • it's used for staking and bonding
  • payment for fees & interoperability

History of Polkadot

The protocol was created by the Ethereum co-founder Dr. Gavin Wood.

He released the first draft of Polkadot White Paper in October 2016.
