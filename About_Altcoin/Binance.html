What is BNB ?

Binance Coin is the cryptocurrency issued by the Binance exchange and trades with the BNB symbol.

It was launched originally on the Ethereum blockchain but later migrated to the Binance Smart Chain, now called BNB Chain.

That means demand for the token is linked to demand for the exchange's services.

Therefore, buying BNB can be seen as a bet on the success of the exchange.

About BNB Chain

Binance Chain and Binance Smart Chain become BNB Chain.

BNB Chain works on Proof-of-Staked Authority (PoSA) consensus mechanism.

Binance Smart Chain (later rebranded BNB Chain) is a layer 1 network, meaning other applications can be built on top of it.

That makes it a competitor of Ethereum.

it offers :-

  • Faster Transactions
  • Cheaper Transaction Fees
  • Better Transaction speeds,

but, it is more centralized.
