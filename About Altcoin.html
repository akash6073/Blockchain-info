What is Polkadot (DOT)

Polkadot is an open source blockchain platform and cryptocurrency.

It is a network protocol that allows arbitrary data—not just tokens—to be transferred across blockchains.

Any type of data across any type of blockchain.

What is Binance Coin (BNB)

Binance coin (BNB) is the exchange token of the Binance crypto exchange.

BNB is a blockchain software system developed by Binance and the community. BNB has multiple forms of utility and powers the Binance Ecosystem as its underlying gas.

Holders of BNB with Binance accounts can access discounted fee on the exchange.
